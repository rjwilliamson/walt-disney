Walt Disney

The Man behind the Mouse

Walt Disney wasn't very good at drawing and he wasn't a very good business man. He has been described as cruel perfectionist and many of the Disney films created in his lifetime went obscenely over budget. However, he is a household name the world over and undoubtedly one of the most sucessful and influential men of all time. He embodies the entrepeurial spirit which captures the imagination of many young people today. He is testimony to looking past failures, persisting through difficulties and attempting what everyone else says is impossible. This makes him the perfect candidate for a tribute page by a new developer just starting out.
